About SIGHT Library

Students can donate their old books to SIGHT Library where their complete record will be kept. These books will be issued for one semester to the juniors, who will be obliged to return them back to SIGHT Library after the end of semester.

The vision of SIGHT Library is to provide some ease to the students who are facing any financial barrier in buying their study related stuff. In university, students have to buy new books in every semester. SIGHT Library is a non-profit project that aims at helping those students by providing them books for a semester without any charges.
